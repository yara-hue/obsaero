# Concept Evaluation Matrix

> **Purpose:** Weighted scoring table used to rank ideas objectively.
> **When to use:** After brainstorming — score the shortlisted concepts here.

### FILE PURPOSE
The objective screening filter used to grade our top ideas mathematically so we pick the absolute best option.

**How to use:** Use our weighted scoring table (Friction, Feasibility, Cost, SDG Alignment) to rate our top 5 ideas. The highest-scoring concept automatically becomes our final project.
