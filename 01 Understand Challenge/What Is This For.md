# 01 Understand Challenge

## Purpose

Understand the official brief AND map our locked concept against it — identify where the concept already scores well and where it has gaps the enrichment stages must fill.

## Inputs

- Competition brief (AeroSTAR‑Boeing)
- Team member availability and strengths

## Process

1. Read the **Competition Brief** to understand the mission and constraints.
2. Review the **Evaluation Rubric** to know how judges will score.
3. Read the **Locked Core Concept** — the concept is pre-submitted and cannot change; understand its fit against the brief.
4. Establish **Team Charter** – roles, commitments, success criteria.
5. Set up **Communication Protocols** – meeting schedules, file naming, plugin config.

## Outputs

- A shared understanding of the challenge.
- Clear awareness of where our locked concept fits the brief and where enrichment is needed.
- Clear team roles and rules.

## Files in this Stage

### Evaluation Rubric
_Purpose:_ The official grading criteria used by Boeing judges — your absolute reference for what matters.

### Official Challenge Brief
_Purpose:_ The competition brief verbatim — every decision traces back here.

### Locked Core Concept
_Purpose:_ The pre-submitted AeroMind AI concept. Locked — we may only enrich and feature around it.

### Team Charter
_Purpose:_ Defines each teammate's role, strengths, and commitment hours.

### Communication Protocols
_Purpose:_ Meeting schedules, sync links, and Obsidian plugin configuration checklist.
