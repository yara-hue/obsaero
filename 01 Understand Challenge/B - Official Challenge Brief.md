## Key Constraints (Extracted)

- Zero new aircraft hardware
- No standalone mobile applications
- Integrate into existing airline workflows and systems
- Zero boarding or operational delay
- Align with SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption & Production) and SDG 13 (Climate Action)
- Focus on behavior, systems, communication, and design — not engineering solutions
