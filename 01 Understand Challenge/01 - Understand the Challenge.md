# 📖 Understand the Challenge

## Purpose

Before you can solve a problem, you must understand it completely. This stage ensures the team aligns on the competition rules, the evaluation criteria, and our internal agreements.

## Inputs

- Competition brief (AeroSTAR‑Boeing)
- Team member availability and strengths

## Process

1. Read the **Competition Brief** to understand the mission and constraints.
2. Review the **Evaluation Rubric** to know how judges will score.
3. Establish **Team Charter** – roles, commitments, success criteria.
4. Set up **Communication Protocols** – meeting schedules, file naming, plugin config.

## Outputs

- A shared understanding of the challenge.
- Clear team roles and rules.
- A plan for the research phase.

## Files in this Stage

### Evaluation Rubric
*Purpose:* The official grading criteria used by Boeing judges – your absolute reference for what matters.

### Team Charter
*Purpose:* Defines each teammate's role, strengths, and commitment hours.

### Communication Protocols
*Purpose:* Meeting schedules, sync links, and Obsidian plugin configuration checklist.
