#  Devil's Advocate

## Purpose

Most ideas fail because nobody tries to break them before the judges do. This stage intentionally attacks every promising concept to expose weaknesses early.

## Inputs

- The winning concept from [[ 04]]
- The [[A - Evaluation Rubric|Evaluation Rubric]] (judge expectations)
- The [[B - Risk Mitigation Register|Risk Mitigation Register]] (to track threats)

## Process

1. **Run structured reviews:**
   - **Airline Executive Review** – will a CFO approve this?
   - **Boeing Judge Review** – does it meet the rubric?
   - **Pre‑Mortem** – imagine we failed; what caused it?
   - **Risk Analysis** – identify operational and passenger friction points.
2. **Generate counterarguments** – write rebuttals for each identified weakness.
3. **Refine the concept** – use the insights to strengthen the blueprint.

## Outputs

- A refined, robust concept with known weaknesses addressed.
- A documented risk register with mitigation strategies.

## Files in this Stage

*Purpose:* Structured prompts to simulate different review panels (copied from the AI manual for convenience).

### Risk Mitigation Register
*Purpose:* Table of every potential rejection reason and its counter‑defense.
