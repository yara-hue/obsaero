#  Concept Blueprint

## Purpose

Turn the refined concept into a complete, detailed system design. This is the master document that describes exactly how the solution works.

## Inputs

- Refined concept from [[ 05]]
- Research data and citations
- Evaluation rubric alignment

## Process

1. **Define the system** – explain the choice architecture, passenger flow, crew interaction.
2. **Specify mechanics** – what changes, when, and why.
3. **Map SDG alignment** – show how at least 2 SDG targets are advanced.
4. **Integrate with operations** – describe how airlines would implement it.
5. **Document** all of this in [[A - Concept Blueprint|Concept Blueprint]] (the file).

## Outputs

- A complete master design document.
