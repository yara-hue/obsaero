# 07 Validation Evidence

## Purpose

Prove every claim made in the enriched locked concept with real‑world evidence. Surveys/interviews test whether the features and framing resonate, and produce evidence for the pitch.

## Inputs

- The [[A - Concept Blueprint|Concept Blueprint]]
- Research from Stage 02
- Any additional data sources

## Process

1. **Review the blueprint** – identify every assertion that needs support.
2. **Find evidence** – pull from [[B - Sources Database|Sources Database]], [[D - Academic Citation Index|Academic Citation Index]], and conduct new interviews or surveys if needed.
3. **Compile an evidence package** – use [[A - Quantitative Survey Data|Quantitative Survey Data]], [[B - User Interview Transcripts|User Interview Transcripts]], and existing citations.
4. **Cross‑reference** – ensure each blueprint claim has a corresponding citation or data point.

## Outputs

- An evidence package that backs every major claim.
- A final check that the solution is grounded in reality.

## Files in this Stage

### Quantitative Survey Data
_Purpose:_ Charts and statistics from any team‑run surveys.

### User Interview Transcripts
_Purpose:_ Raw notes or transcripts from passenger/crew interviews, with direct quotes for credibility.

### Academic Citation Index
_Purpose:_ Links each blueprint feature to peer‑reviewed principles.
