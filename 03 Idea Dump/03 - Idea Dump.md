# 💡 Idea Dump

## Purpose

Generate as many ideas as possible – without judging them. Quantity leads to quality. This is the divergent phase.

## Inputs

- Research insights and data
- Understanding of the problem space

## Process

1. **Brainstorm** – use [[A - Idea Pool|Idea Pool]] (the file) to capture every idea, no matter how wild.
   - Target: 100+ concepts.
   - Categorise by theme (terminal operations, cabin behaviour, communication, etc.).
2. **Don't filter** – at this stage, all ideas are welcome.
3. **Use AI** – leverage prompts from the `60_AI_Prompt_Engineering` folder to generate variations.

## Outputs

- A large pool of raw concepts ready for evaluation.
