## 📌 Critical Flight Safety Briefing (Our North Stars)
1. **Zero New Hardware:** If a concept requires a new physical engineering component or advanced aircraft design, it is disqualified. We focus entirely on systems, communication, and human habits.
2. **Operational Integration:** Our system must integrate cleanly into existing touchpoints (booking apps, kiosks, or boarding rituals) without causing ground or staff delays.
3. **Double SDG Anchor:** Every proposal must explicitly prove how it advances targets within at least two chosen UN SDGs.
