### ?? FILE PURPOSE
The emotional and structural outline of our presentation, built around the "Hero's Journey" framework.
*   **How to use:** Draft the story arc here before writing individual slides. Map out where the hook is, where the data drops, and where the SDG impact is revealed.

---

## Hero's Journey Arc

### Hook (0:00 - 0:30)
_Open with a surprising fact, story, or question._
- _e.g. "Every year, 6 million kilograms of waste are generated in airplane cabins — and 80% of it goes to landfill."_

### Problem (0:30 - 1:30)
_Define the problem in human terms._
- _e.g. "Passengers WANT to recycle, but they can't — the bins are confusing, the bins are unlabeled, and crew don't have time to explain."_

### Solution Reveal (1:30 - 3:00)
_Introduce the concept as the answer._
- _e.g. "What if every seat became a smart sorting station?"_

### Data Drop (3:00 - 4:00)
_Hit them with numbers._
- _e.g. Survey results, waste reduction percentages, cost savings_

### SDG Impact Reveal (4:00 - 4:30)
_Connect to the bigger picture._
- _e.g. "This directly addresses SDG 12 and SDG 13 — and here's how."_

### Call to Action (4:30 - 5:00)
_Close with urgency._
- _e.g. "Airlines are ready. Passengers are ready. The only thing missing is this system."_
