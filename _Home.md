## How We Work

Work alternates between **individual sprints** and **team syncs**.

| Stage | Mode | Who | Team Sync |
|:------|:-----|:----|:----------|
| [[01 Understand Challenge/What Is This For|01]] | Individual | Map locked concept against brief | Align as team |
| [[02 Research/What Is This For|02]] | Individual | Targeted research around locked concept | Share findings |
| [[03 Feature Expansion/What Is This For|03]] | Individual | Diverge on features around the locked concept | Pool & discuss |
| [[04 Feature Evaluation/What Is This For|04]] | Team | Score & select features | — |
| [[05 Devils Advocate/What Is This For|05]] | Team | Critical quality gate — bulletproof the concept | — |
| [[06 Concept Blueprint/What Is This For|06]] | Individual | Assemble enriched concept per module | Merge into master doc |
| [[07 Validation Evidence/What Is This For|07]] | Individual | Prove every claim of enriched concept | Cross-check evidence |
| [[08 Pitch Studio/What Is This For|08]] | Team | Build the presentation story together | — |
| [[09 Final Deliverables/What Is This For|09]] | Team | Final review & submit | — |

> [!important] **Connection points**
> After stages **01, 02, 03, 06, 07** the team meets to merge individual work before moving forward.

## The Journey

1. **[[01 Understand Challenge/What Is This For|01]]** — Understand the brief AND map the locked concept against it.
2. **[[02 Research/What Is This For|02]]** — Targeted research that serves the locked concept.
3. **[[03 Feature Expansion/What Is This For|03]]** — Diverge on features around the locked concept.
4. **[[04 Feature Evaluation/What Is This For|04]]** — Score and select the strongest features.
5. **[[05 Devils Advocate/What Is This For|05]]** — Quality gate: stress-test until bulletproof.
6. **[[06 Concept Blueprint/What Is This For|06]]** — Assemble enriched concept into a complete design.
7. **[[07 Validation Evidence/What Is This For|07]]** — Prove every claim with evidence.
8. **[[08 Pitch Studio/What Is This For|08]]** — Craft a compelling story.
9. **[[09 Final Deliverables/What Is This For|09]]** — Submit the final package.
