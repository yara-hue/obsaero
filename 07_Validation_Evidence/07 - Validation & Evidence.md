# ✅ Validation & Evidence

## Purpose

Prove every claim made in the blueprint with real‑world evidence. Judges will look for data, not just stories.

## Inputs

- The [[Concept Blueprint]]
- Research from Stage 02
- Any additional data sources

## Process

1. **Review the blueprint** – identify every assertion that needs support.
2. **Find evidence** – pull from [[Sources Database]], [[Academic Citation Index]], and conduct new interviews or surveys if needed.
3. **Compile an evidence package** – use [[Quantitative Survey Data]], [[User Interview Transcripts]], and existing citations.
4. **Cross‑reference** – ensure each blueprint claim has a corresponding citation or data point.

## Outputs

- An evidence package that backs every major claim.
- A final check that the solution is grounded in reality.

## Files in this Stage

### Quantitative Survey Data
*Purpose:* Charts and statistics from any team‑run surveys.

### User Interview Transcripts
*Purpose:* Raw notes or transcripts from passenger/crew interviews, with direct quotes for credibility.

### Academic Citation Index
*Purpose:* Links each blueprint feature to peer‑reviewed principles.
